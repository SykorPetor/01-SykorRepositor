Stardew Valley is an open-ended country-life RPG developed by ConcernedApe. Players take the role of a character who inherits their deceased grandfather's dilapidated farm in a place known as Stardew Valley. The game was released for Windows in February 2016 before being ported to other platforms. In Stardew Valley, players can choose to focus on a variety of activities, including farming, fishing, mining, foraging, and crafting. They can also choose to build relationships with the townsfolk, who each have their own unique stories and personalities.
